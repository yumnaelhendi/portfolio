Anthony’s Pizza

Classy and welcoming brand redesign

Website Design Brand Redesign Logo Design Group Project

Project Scope

This group project focused on creating a clean, classy, and welcoming website for Anthony’s Pizza while improving navigation and strengthening the brand’s visual identity.

I contributed to a redesign direction that better communicates the restaurant’s energy and supports a more refined, gourmet feel. The logo uses a wordmark approach with a pizza icon to reinforce that tone.

  • Classy and gourmet logo direction
  • Website layouts designed for clearer navigation
  • Brand refresh built around warmth and appetite appeal

Website mockups and brand applications created to feel welcoming, polished, and food-focused.

Visual Language

The project combines food imagery, warm tones, and clean structure to build an inviting experience. I wanted the brand to feel elevated while still familiar and approachable.

Design Goal

The objective was to give the restaurant a stronger digital presence and a more memorable identity that feels both premium and accessible to its audience.

Outcome

This project demonstrates branding, layout design, and collaborative problem-solving in a hospitality setting, with a focus on both user navigation and visual appetite appeal.
