Project Overview

KatMat Productions is a branding and digital identity project focused on building a stronger, more contemporary visual presence for a media production company. The goal was to refine the brand, modernize the logo, and create a system that feels professional, clear, and memorable.

The branding guide defines the brand as assertive, professional, approachable, and innovative, with communication centered on creativity, visual storytelling, and client success.

Communication Goals

The branding guide outlines several communication goals for KatMat. These include elevating visual storytelling, supporting client success, promoting accessibility and inclusivity, showcasing innovative creativity, and highlighting Canadian roots with two decades of expertise.

Brand Mission & Vision

The guide describes KatMat Media as dedicated to the art of visual storytelling, creating compelling narratives that engage, inspire, and captivate. It also presents a vision of creativity without limits, shaping a brighter and more connected future through media.

Color Palette

The guide explains that the palette uses a strong red, rich black, light grey, off-white, and stark white to create a minimal, high-contrast identity. It also notes that the creative side uses all five colors, while the corporate side relies more on black, grey, and whites.

Outcome

This project helped me develop a full brand direction rather than just a logo. By combining identity design, messaging, color strategy, and typography, I created a stronger system that gives KatMat Productions a more confident and cohesive professional presence.
