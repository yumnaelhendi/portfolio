Side by Side App

Meditation and stress-management experience

UX/UI Figma Research Mobile Design

Project Objective

I led a team in designing the “Meditate” section of the Side by Side app. The goal was to create a supportive, user-friendly space that helps users manage stress through assessments, coping strategies, and guided exercises.

This part of the app was designed to feel calm, clear, and approachable while helping users navigate emotional stress through mindful practice and structured support.

  • Focused on stress relief and mindful guidance
  • Designed for clear navigation and gentle user flow
  • Balanced supportive content with visual calm

Main interface screen for the Meditate section, including guided actions and assessment tools.

Design Approach

I focused on a soft visual language and a simple structure to create calm while guiding the experience without feeling overwhelming. Clear hierarchy, nurturing copy, and accessible navigation were key decisions in the interface.

What I Contributed

I worked on the concept direction, screen design, and the structure of the meditation section. The work centered on creating an experience that feels emotionally supportive while still practical and usable.
