How Trends Go Viral

Infographic exploring how online trends spread and gain momentum

Infographic Graphic Design Research Visual Hierarchy

Project Objective

This project explores how trends spread across digital platforms and why certain ideas gain rapid attention. The goal was to translate a broad topic into a visual format that is clear, engaging, and easy to follow.

I focused on organizing information in a way that feels dynamic but still readable, using hierarchy, pacing, and strong visual structure to guide the viewer.

  • Transforms research into a visually engaging format
  • Uses clear hierarchy to guide the reader through the topic
  • Balances data, design, and storytelling in one layout

Final infographic design showing how trends move through attention, sharing, and audience behavior.

Design Approach

I approached the infographic by breaking the topic into smaller, digestible sections and arranging them in a logical visual flow. Typography, spacing, and color contrast were used to keep the layout energetic without becoming overwhelming.

What I Focused On

My main focus was clarity and engagement. I wanted the infographic to feel informative while still visually exciting, making the content easier to understand and more memorable.

Outcome

This project strengthened my ability to turn research-heavy content into visual storytelling. It reflects my interest in creating design work that communicates clearly while still feeling polished, current, and audience-focused.
